About

I'm a PhD student enroled in the University of Salamanca and ICTJA-CSIC.

I started my PhD in January 2016. The project is entitled, The central system and the deformation mechanisms of the Iberian Peninsula. The aim of the project is to study the NW part of the Iberian Massif by means of a wide-angle seismic experiment.

Additional geophyisical data will be used, e.g. magnetics, gravity, to further constrain the lithospheric structure of this portion of the Variscan Domain.

Currently I'm working on estimating the depth to the Curie-Isotherm for the Iberian Microplate.
